Azure is Microsoft's cloud computing platform. 
It offers a variety of services like virtual machines, web hosting, databases, AI tools, and more. 
It lets businesses build, deploy, and manage applications and services without managing physical servers. 
Azure is known for its global reach, security features, and scalability.
Learn more about [Azure](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/)
